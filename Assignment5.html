My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. The Shawshank Redemption
    • Year Released: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
    • A gripping tale of friendship and redemption in a prison setting.
  2. Pulp Fiction
    • Year Released: 1994
    • Main Actors: John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson
    • A stylish and unconventional crime film with intersecting storylines.
  3. Inception
    • Year Released: 2010
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt
    • A mind-bending sci-fi thriller about dreams within dreams.
  4. Forrest Gump
    • Year Released: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tom Hanks, Robin Wright
    • A heartwarming story of an ordinary man's extraordinary life.
  5. The Dark Knight
    • Year Released: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger
    • An intense superhero film with an iconic portrayal of the Joker.
